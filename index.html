I'm a principal software engineer at the Allen Institute for AI (Ai2), where I work on the OLMo family of language models. My work spans the full training pipeline — from pretraining data curation and mixing to evaluation — with a focus on making large-scale language model development reproducible and transparent.

My broader interests are in how capabilities emerge during training and in the evaluation infrastructure necessary to measure them — especially as models take on more agentic tasks involving tool use and sophisticated multi-step reasoning.
